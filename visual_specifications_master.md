# VISUAL SPECIFICATIONS FOR "THE FORGOTTEN KINGS"
## A Comprehensive Guide for Historical Thriller Illustrations (1000-1758 CE)

*These specifications provide detailed guidance for creating visually compelling and historically accurate illustrations that enhance the thriller narrative while maintaining scholarly rigor.*

---

## GENERAL VISUAL PRINCIPLES

### Thriller Aesthetic Guidelines
- **Color Palette**: Rich, dramatic colors with strong contrasts - deep crimsons for blood, golden yellows for power, shadow blacks for intrigue
- **Lighting**: Dramatic chiaroscuro effects emphasizing tension and mystery
- **Composition**: Dynamic angles that suggest movement, conflict, and hidden dangers
- **Details**: Focus on elements that suggest violence, strategy, and political intrigue - bloodstained weapons, calculating expressions, architectural elements that hint at defensive capabilities

### Historical Accuracy Standards
- All clothing, weapons, and architectural elements must be period-accurate
- Regional variations in art styles, building techniques, and cultural symbols must be respected
- Military technology and tactics should reflect documented historical practices
- Geographic features and territorial boundaries should align with contemporary sources
