# BOOK COVER SPECIFICATIONS: "THE FORGOTTEN KINGS"

## General Aesthetic
**Genre:** Historical Thriller / Narrative Non-Fiction
**Tone:** Dark, cinematic, mysterious, epic, gritty.
**Color Palette:** Deep blacks, shadows, metallic gold, blood red, parchment beige.
**Typography:** Strong, serif fonts (e.g., Trajan, Cinzel) with metallic textures.

---

## FRONT COVER

### Visual Concept: "The Weight of History"
**Central Image:**
A dramatic, high-contrast close-up of an ancient, weathered map of India spread across a dark wooden table. The map is not pristine; it is torn, stained with ink and blood.

**Key Elements:**
1.  **The Crown:** A heavy, ornate Indian royal crown (generic but historically plausible) sits on the map, but it is slightly tilted or cracked, suggesting the fragility of power.
2.  **The Weapons:** A curved sword (Tulwar or Khanda) and a quill pen lie crossed beneath the crown. The sword's edge glints in the light; the quill is dripping fresh ink (or blood).
3.  **Lighting:** The scene is lit by a single, unseen oil lamp from the side, creating deep *chiaroscuro* shadows. The light catches the gold of the crown and the wet ink/blood on the map.
4.  **Atmosphere:** Dust motes dancing in the light. A sense of secrets hidden in the shadows.

### Typography
*   **Title:** **THE FORGOTTEN KINGS**
    *   *Style:* Large, imposing, metallic gold with a weathered/tarnished texture. Embossed effect.
    *   *Placement:* Top 1/3 of the cover.
*   **Subtitle:** **Ten Rulers Who Shaped the Destiny of India**
    *   *Style:* Smaller, elegant sans-serif or thinner serif. White or pale gold.
    *   *Placement:* Just below the title.
*   **Author Name:** **ROSHAN VENUGOPAL**
    *   *Style:* Clean, bold, white.
    *   *Placement:* Bottom center.
