## Research Methodology Notes

**Search Strategy Used:**
- Targeted queries for specific historical events and figures
- Cross-verification using multiple academic and historical sources
- Focus on primary source documentation where available (Inscriptions, Chronicles)
- Contemporary chronicle verification (e.g., *Madala Panji*, *Samarangana Sutradhara*)

**Source Quality Assessment:**
- Wikipedia: Used as starting point, cross-verified with academic sources
- Academic institutions: University and research papers prioritized
- Government sources: Archaeological Survey and cultural ministry sites
- Historical societies: Specialized historical organization publications

**Verification Standards:**
- Multiple source confirmation required for all major claims
- Contemporary vs. later source evaluation
- Archaeological evidence correlation where available
- Scholarly consensus documentation
