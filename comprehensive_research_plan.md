# Comprehensive Research Plan: Blood and Thrones

## Overview
This research plan aims to enhance the historical accuracy and depth of "Blood and Thrones: The Unknown Kings Who Forged India" by conducting systematic research on each ruler using primary sources, academic papers, and specialized historical databases.

## Research Methodology
1. **Primary Sources**: Original chronicles, inscriptions, court records
2. **Secondary Academic Sources**: Peer-reviewed journals, university publications
3. **Archaeological Evidence**: Inscriptions, monuments, artifacts
4. **Contemporary Accounts**: Foreign travelers, diplomatic records
5. **Modern Scholarship**: Recent historical research and analysis
